Quickstart

Single completion

from scitex_genai import GenAI

ai = GenAI(model="gpt-4o-mini")
print(ai("Explain neural networks in one sentence."))
print("cost USD:", ai.cost)

Switch providers without changing the call

ai = GenAI(model="claude-sonnet-4-6")
ai("Same call, different backend.")

The GenAI factory dispatches to the matching provider class inside scitex_genai.llm (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Groq, DeepSeek, Perplexity, Llama). Cost tracking and conversation history are provider-agnostic.

For a runnable walk-through see examples/01_genai.ipynb.
